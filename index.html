✏️ About Us

Small school.
Real work. Every day.

★Savong Education is a community-based school in rural Cambodia. We provide free English classes, sports, and practical life skills for children who would otherwise have no access to them.

Most of our students come from families with very little. Our job is straightforward: give them skills they can use.

We are not a large organization. No complex systems, no outside management. We teach, we show up, and we keep the doors open every single day.

"A student who learns English, plays, and builds confidence has a completely different future."
— Savong Education, Prasat Bakong, Siem Reap
⭐ Director & Founder
🏫 Our Story

Meet Vong Svay

Director · Sole Sponsor · Siem Reap Local

When the previous founder closed Savong School, the children of Prasat Bakong had nowhere to go. Vong Svay decided to do something about it.

A small business owner from Siem Reap, Vong had no prior connection to the school. He heard what had happened, stepped in, and has been funding and running it out of his own pocket ever since. No grants. No organization behind him.

Today he serves as Director and remains the school's only financial backer. The school is open because of him.

"The children needed someone to show up. So I did."
📖 What We Teach

Three pillars. One purpose.

01

English

Basic to intermediate English, taught in small groups. Students learn to speak, read, and write at their own pace.

  • Speaking and listening practice
  • Reading and writing basics
  • Skills that open up better jobs
02

Sports

Kids need to move. Sports give them an outlet, a reason to come back, and something to work toward together.

  • Teamwork and fair play
  • Physical health and energy
  • Confidence outside the classroom
03

Life Skills

Practical things school doesn't always teach. Habits and awareness that stay with students as they get older.

  • How to communicate clearly
  • Hygiene and daily habits
  • Knowing your own value
💡 Why It Matters

In rural areas, opportunity is limited.

Free
All programs, no cost to families
Daily
Consistent teaching, every day
Rural
Serving children with no other access
Local
Community-based, Cambodian-led

Without school, most of these children have nowhere to go during the day and no clear path forward. Job options are limited. Options, full stop, are limited.

A few hours of English per week changes what is possible for a child here. It is not complicated.

Every class, every game, every conversation in English adds up. We have watched it happen.

✈️ Volunteer

Come teach. Come help.
Make it real.

Savong Education is open to volunteers from anywhere in the world. If you have a week, two weeks, or longer, come and teach. The students are eager, the team is welcoming, and the work is straightforward.

No teaching degree needed. If you can speak English and show up on time, we can work with you.

1

Get in touch

Send us a message with a bit about yourself and when you are available. We will get back to you within a few days.

2

Plan your visit

The school is in Prasat Bakong, about 25km from Siem Reap town. We can help point you toward local accommodation.

3

Show up and teach

Meet the students, get a feel for the classroom, and start. There is no complicated onboarding process.

🌏 All nationalities welcome
📅 Flexible duration
🏫 English teaching focus
🤝 Local team support
